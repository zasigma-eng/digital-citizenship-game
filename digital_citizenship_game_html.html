Why this matters

A lower offensive rate shows stronger digital citizenship. Careful communication supports safer, more respectful online communities.

Risk of offensive choices

Reflection

Before posting, ask: Is it true? Is it necessary? Is it kind? The internet remembers more than we think, so cautious and considerate choices matter.
